# Construction ERP Mobile Application

## Product Overview

A React Native mobile application for construction site management serving three distinct user roles: Workers, Supervisors, and Drivers. The app provides comprehensive construction project management capabilities including attendance tracking, task management, daily reporting, and request processing.

## Core Features

- **Role-Based Access Control**: Distinct interfaces and permissions for Workers, Supervisors, and Drivers
- **Location-Based Attendance**: GPS and geofencing integration for accurate work hour tracking
- **Task Management**: Sequential task assignments with dependency validation and progress tracking
- **Daily Reporting**: Photo-enabled work reports with issue tracking and supervisor communication
- **Request Management**: Leave requests, material requests, advance payments, and reimbursements
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Push notifications for task updates, approvals, and site changes
- **Offline Support**: Cached data access and action queuing for areas with poor connectivity

## Target Users

- **Construction Workers**: Field workers who need to track attendance, complete tasks, and submit reports
- **Site Supervisors**: Management personnel who oversee workers, approve requests, and monitor progress
- **Drivers**: Transportation staff who handle logistics and material delivery

## Key Business Requirements

- **Field-Optimized UX**: Large touch targets, high contrast design, minimal typing for gloved hands
- **Location Accuracy**: Precise GPS tracking with geofence validation for attendance integrity
- **Backend Integration**: API-first approach with all business logic handled by existing backend system
- **Compliance**: Work pass tracking, certification management, and safety incident reporting
- **Connectivity Resilience**: Graceful degradation in poor network conditions with offline capabilities

## Success Metrics

- Accurate attendance tracking with location validation
- Streamlined task completion and progress reporting
- Efficient request processing and approval workflows
- Reduced administrative overhead through mobile automation
- Improved communication between field workers and supervisors
